-Hi, Sarah. -Good morning, Mike. Today is so warm. 翻译：—嗨，萨拉。 —早上好，迈克。今天真暖和。

Let's go swimming. -Sorry, I can't. I have to do my homework now. 翻译：我们去游泳吧。 —对不起，我不能去游泳。我现在得做作业。

Let's check 翻译：让我们检查一下

Listen and tick. 翻译：听录音，并打勾。

1. -Hi, John. What are you going to do tomorrow? 翻译：1. —嗨，约翰。你明天打算做什么？

-Not much. I'm just going to do my homework and buy a postcard tomorrow morning. 翻译： —不多。我明天早上要做作业然后买张明信片。

And you? -I'm going to see a film tomorrow afternoon. 翻译：你呢？ —明天下午我要去看电影。

-Great! Can I go, too? 翻译： —太好了！我也能去吗？

Question: What is John going to do tomorrow morning? 翻译： 问题：约翰明天早上要做什么？

2. -Amy, did you see my new comic book? 翻译：2. —艾米，你看到我的新漫画书了吗？

I'm going to read it this evening. -Oh! 翻译：我今晚要读它。 —哦！

-Yes. I can give it to you tomorrow. -OK. Thanks. 翻译： —可以。我可以明天给你。 —好啊。谢谢。

Question: What is the boy going to do this evening? 翻译： 问题：这个男孩今晚要做什么？

3. -I'm going to the bookstore to buy a new dictionary. 翻译：3.—我打算去书店买一本新的字典。

-Oh! When are you going? -This afternoon. 翻译： —哦！你什么时候去？ —今天下午。

The bookstore isn't open this evening. Question: Where is the boy going? 翻译：书店今天傍晚不营业。 问题：那男孩要去哪里？

4. -I'm going to buy some milk and a newspaper. 翻译：4. —我打算买些牛奶和报纸。

-Where are you going to buy them? 翻译： —你打算在哪里买？

-I'm going to buy them at the small shop near the post office. 翻译： —我打算在邮局附近的小店买。

-Really? I'm going to buy a postcard there. 翻译： —真的吗？我打算在那里买张明信片。

Can I go with you? -Sure! Let's go together! 翻译：我能和你一起去吗？ —当然！我们一起去吧！

Question: What is the woman going to buy? 翻译： 问题：这个女人要买什么？
